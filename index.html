About

I started making furniture to pass time during quarantine but it quickly grew into a hobby. I try to use mostly recycled materials and sll of the wood from the products comes from the Ohio university Wood Compost and the metal from various dump sites. Since the materials are recycled, all pieces are one-of-one. Contact me for your custom furniture piece!
